Case Study Brief

GHOST
SUBSCRIPTIONS.

Users consistently lose capital to forgotten recurring payments. LockboxIQ (formerly SubscriptionIQ) was engineered as a native extension of the Pulse engine to automatically isolate, track, and predict subscription impacts on the 90-day trajectory.

Automated Discovery

Leverages Plaid telemetry to detect recurring vendor patterns before they become long-term liabilities.

Capital Protection

Integrates directly with Pulse's 'Predicted End' balance forecasting to alert users of upcoming heavy subscription weeks.

Engineering Principles

THE INTEGRITY ENGINE.

Unified Authentication

Engineered as a standalone program and an unlockable bundle feature in Pulse. Users share a single, secure credential across both systems for a frictionless experience.

Data Integrity

Categorical to Ordinal processing isolates subscription data without polluting standard transaction views.

Behavioral Sync

Nova AI monitors these locked expenses to provide personalized recommendations on subscription pruning.

PostgreSQL

Persistent relational schema linking users to recurring fact_transactions.

Plaid API

Secure, read-only telemetry for recurring charge detection.

Nova Logic

Grouped Imputation ensures missing subscription dates are accurately projected.

Bundle Architecture

Modular component design allowing seamless integration into the Pulse UI.

The problem

Subscription chaos is costing you more than money.

Bills stack up. Passwords get scattered. And the moment something goes wrong with a card or a login, you have no single place to go for answers.

🚨

"My card just got compromised."

You need to update your payment info across every active subscription — but you don't know which ones are on that card. It's a 45-minute scramble through bank statements that should never have to happen.

"Is Hulu on the Visa or the Chase card? Was that gym membership auto-renewed? What else is on there?"
💸

"Wait — I'm still paying for that?"

A streaming service you stopped watching six months ago. A software trial that quietly became $19.99/month. A gym membership from a city you no longer live in. Subscriptions are designed to be easy to forget.

"I just found three charges I didn't recognize. I've been paying for them for almost a year."
🔑

"What was my password again?"

You need to cancel a service but can't get into the account. The password is saved on a browser you no longer use, or in a note app you can't find. The reset email goes to an old address. It snowballs fast.

"I just spent 20 minutes trying to cancel one subscription because I couldn't log in."
What Orbit does

Three layers of control in one app.

Orbit isn't just a subscription list. It's the complete picture of what you're paying, how you're paying for it, and how to get into it when you need to.

Layer 01
📋

Subscription & bill tracker

Every recurring charge in one view. Name, cost, billing cycle, next renewal date, and category. Orbit auto-detects subscriptions from your connected bank and card accounts — so you see what's actually billing you, not just what you remember signing up for.

Auto-detection via Plaid
Layer 02
💳

Payment method mapping

See exactly which card or account is tied to each subscription and bill. Filter your entire list by payment method in one tap. When a card gets compromised or expires, you know in seconds what needs updating — no bank statement archaeology required.

Compromised card? Fixed in seconds.
Layer 03
🔒

Built-in password vault

Attach the login credentials for each service directly to its entry in Orbit. AES-256 encrypted with zero-knowledge architecture — you're the only one who can see your passwords. No browser dependency, no password sticky notes, no reset email chase.

Zero-knowledge encryption
How it works

Set up in minutes. Useful for life.

Orbit gets out of your way fast. Connect once, organize once — then it runs quietly in the background, alerting you before things happen instead of after.

1

Connect your accounts

Link your bank accounts and credit cards securely via Plaid. Orbit scans your transaction history and auto-identifies every recurring charge — the ones you know about and the ones you forgot.

2

Attach your credentials

Add the login for each subscription right inside its entry. Everything is encrypted before it leaves your device. One tap to reveal when you need it. Invisible and inaccessible to everyone else, including us.

3

Stay ahead of everything

Get renewal alerts before charges hit. Filter by card to see your exposure instantly. Catch spending you've forgotten about. Cancel what you don't need. You're in control — finally.
